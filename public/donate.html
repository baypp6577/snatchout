Frequently Asked Questions

Where does my donation go?
Your donation covers event expenses including venue rental, sound equipment, guest minister honorariums, and refreshments - all to make the Gospel accessible to everyone.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
SnatchOut is a faith-based event. Please consult with your tax advisor regarding the deductibility of charitable donations in your jurisdiction.
Can I donate by other methods?
Currently we accept donations through PayPal. For other donation methods, please contact us at info@snatchout.com.
