Frequently Asked Questions

Where does my donation go?
Your donation covers event expenses including venue rental, sound equipment, guest minister honorariums, travel costs, and refreshments - all to make the Gospel accessible to everyone.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
SnatchOut is a project of Evangelists for Christ Ministries, a registered charity (Charity Number: 1137360). UK taxpayers can claim tax relief on charitable donations through: Self Assessment tax returns, contacting HMRC to adjust tax code, or via their tax advisor.
Can I donate by other methods?
Currently we accept donations through PayPal. Additional payment methods will be available soon.
